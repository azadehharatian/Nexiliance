Key Milestones

CRA Enforcement Timeline

The CRA entered into force December 2024. Here are the critical upcoming deadlines.

✅
December 2024 — Complete
CRA Enters into Force
The EU Cyber Resilience Act officially entered into force. The compliance preparation period has begun.
⚡
September 2026 — Upcoming
Vulnerability & Incident Reporting Obligations
Manufacturers must comply with ENISA vulnerability reporting. Actively exploited vulnerabilities must be reported within 24 hours.
🎯
December 2027 — Full Enforcement
All CRA Requirements Apply — CE Marking Required
Products without CRA-compliant CE marking cannot be placed on the EU market. Non-compliance penalties up to €15M or 2.5% of global annual turnover.
